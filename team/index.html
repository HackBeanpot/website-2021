Meet the team behind HackBeanpot

We are a group of ~20 driven students who aim to make technical experience and knowledge accessible to anyone who's interested. In the months leading up to our hackathon in February, we work hard both within and across our five teams - Design, Outreach, Social Media, Sponsorship, and Tech. Meet them below!
